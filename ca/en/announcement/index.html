Policy & Service Updates

+ Service Updates

We ask that all users of the TaskRabbit platform continue to stay aware of the recommendations from relevant authorities in your location and neither submit nor accept job requests that are in violation of local, state, provincial, or federal guidance. Taskers and clients who violate applicable orders or guidance may have their account suspended from the platform. To see the latest updates to TaskRabbit service modifications in your city, please visit here.

+ What to Do If You’re Experiencing Symptoms

If you have been diagnosed with COVID-19, have reason to believe you have COVID-19, or have been exposed to someone with COVID-19, please notify TaskRabbit promptly here. If you are a Tasker, you can also reach us via live chat in the Support section of the Tasker app.

Symptoms of illness may include:
  • • Cough
  • • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • • Fever
Or any two of these symptoms:
  • • Chilss
  • • Repeated shaking due to chills
  • • Muscle pains
  • • Headache
  • • Sore throat
  • • New loss of taste or smell
+ Responsible Information Sharing

If a client or a Tasker tells us that they have been diagnosed with or have some symptoms consistent with COVID-19, we may contact other users they have been in contact with so that they can take appropriate steps to safeguard their health and the health of those around them. Equally, if a client or a Tasker expresses a concern that another user may have COVID-19, we will contact said user and update the client or Tasker who raised the concern initially. We may temporarily pause the accounts of users who have been diagnosed with or exposed to COVID-19, consistent with public health quarantining guidelines. For more information on how we use personal information and how you can exercise your rights over your personal information, please see here.

Important Information for Clients

+ Waiving Cancellation Fees

We will waive cancellation fees for all clients who cancel a task within 24 hours of the scheduled start time due to symptoms of COVID-19. To make a change to your tasks, visit the My Tasks section of the TaskRabbit website.

+ Contactless & Virtual Tasks

As restrictions are lifted across regions and cities, we ask the TaskRabbit community to remain mindful of all public health guidance and regulations in your area, especially since they may change from one day to the next. If your area allows in-person contact, please feel free to book these tasks. However, if a task can be done in a contactless or virtual way, we encourage taking these precautions.

A contactless task is one that maintains social distancing of at least 2 metres from another person. For example, you can request your Tasker perform the task outside, in a separate room, or offsite (e.g. leaving a delivery at your door). Instructions for contactless tasks can be indicated in the booking details and/or via task chat.

You may also request that a task be done virtually via chat, a call, or video. For example, you can have a Tasker complete data entry or personal assistant work virtually or guide you through a furniture assembly via a video call. Just let your Tasker know in your task description how you’d like to complete the task virtually.

If you are experiencing symptoms of illness, please let your Tasker know. Both you and your Tasker should take appropriate precautions to perform the task in a contactless or virtual way.

+ Free Volunteer Help through Tasks for Good

Tasks for Good connects vulnerable individuals and organizations in need with local volunteer Taskers who donate their time and skills to support their communities. Through Tasks for Good, a volunteer Tasker can pick up medications, deliver meals, drop off supplies, and more. To learn more about the program, including eligibility and availability in your city, visit here.

Volunteer help is intended for individuals severely impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic (immunocompromised, elderly, disabled, healthcare workers, etc.) and organizations (non-profits, government agencies, community-based organizations, local small businesses) facing financial hardship and in need of volunteer support. We kindly encourage anyone not in a vulnerable position to hire Taskers in TaskRabbit’s general marketplace.

If you’re looking for a volunteer, visit this page to find a volunteer Tasker near you. If you’re looking to task as a volunteer through Tasks for Good, visit here to download and register through the Tasker app.
