We’re committed to safely helping neighbours connect for help with everyday life.

In order to protect the TaskRabbit community at this time, all tasks are currently contactless. You and your Tasker must agree to:

  • Maintain a safe separation of six feet/two meters (social distancing)
  • Follow all applicable public health guidelines and regulations.

Thanks for doing your part to ensure the health of your community! See what else we’re doing to protect the TaskRabbit community.

How do contactless tasks work?

1. Add instructions in task details at booking, such as drop-off location or other logistics.

2. Confirm details in chat. Your Tasker will reach out with any question

3. Tasker gets the job done! They’ll let you know when they’ve arrived and completed the task.
